What’s BMI?

BMI compares your weight to your height, and is calculated
by dividing your weight (in kilograms) by your height(in
metres squared). It gives you an idea of whether you’re
'underweight', a 'healthy' weight, 'overweight', or 'obese' for
your height. BMI isone type of tool to help health
professionals assess the risk for chronic disease.



# BMI Weight Status
1 Below 18 underweight
2 18-25 Normal Weight
3 25-30 Over Weight
4 above 30 Obese








How to calculate BMI

Body mass index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height
and weight that applies to adult men and women.
